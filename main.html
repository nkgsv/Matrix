Simplify matrix online!

Drag and drop the elements of the matrix to perform elementary operations with rows or columns. Examples:

  • Drag the first element of the first row and drop it to the first element of the second row. The first row will be added to the second one. (α=+1)
  • Drag the second element of the third column to the second element of the first column. The third column will be added to the first one. (α=+1)
  • Hold ALT to subtract instead of adding. (α=-1)
  • Hold SHIFT to compute the multiplier α in such a way that the target element becomes zero.
  • Hold CTRL to move the selected row or column.
  • Enter a number on the right of a row and hit ENTER to multipy the row by this number. E.g. enter "1/2" to divide by 2.
  • Similar for columns.
  • Click "Edit matrix" and change the values of the elements. Then click "Lock matrix".
  • Do not use Internet Explorer.
